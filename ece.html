About the Program

Our UG ECE program is designed to equip students with in-depth knowledge in electronics, communication systems, embedded technologies, and signal processing. Students learn to innovate, design, and implement electronic systems that power our modern digital world.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To produce globally competent engineers in the field of electronics and communication.

Mission: To provide quality education, foster research and innovation, and prepare students to meet industrial and societal needs.

Program Highlights

  • Analog & Digital Circuit Design
  • VLSI and Embedded Systems
  • Signal Processing & Communication
  • Robotics and IoT
  • Control Systems and Automation
  • Wireless & Satellite Communication

Skills Developed

  • Hardware Programming
  • PCB and Circuit Simulation
  • Embedded C / IoT Solutions
  • Communication System Analysis
  • Electromagnetics and RF Design
  • Project-Based Learning

Course Structure

The ECE program includes foundational courses in electronics and mathematics, core subjects like microprocessors, analog/digital communication, and advanced electives such as IoT, AI for electronics, and VLSI Design.
