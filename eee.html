About the Program

UG in EEE focuses on the design, development, and maintenance of electrical systems and electronics. It blends core concepts of power generation, electrical machines, and electronics with modern applications in automation, renewable energy, and smart grids.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a center of excellence in electrical and electronics education and research.

Mission: To develop technically skilled and ethically responsible engineers ready to lead in the global energy and automation industry.

Program Highlights

  • Power Systems & Distribution
  • Electrical Machines & Drives
  • Industrial Automation & Control
  • Digital Electronics
  • Smart Grids & Renewable Energy
  • Electrical CAD & Simulation

Skills Developed

  • Power System Design
  • Control Systems Engineering
  • PLC, SCADA & Automation
  • Electrical Safety & Energy Auditing
  • Circuit Simulation (PSCAD, MATLAB)
  • Embedded System Basics

Course Structure

The curriculum includes core electrical subjects, practical labs, and electives like electric vehicles, green energy systems, and industrial IoT. Projects and internships ensure hands-on industry experience.
