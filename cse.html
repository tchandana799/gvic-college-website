B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering

UG CSE offers a future-proof path into computing, AI, data science, and much more.

ABOUT UG CSE

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a 4-year undergraduate program that focuses on the theoretical foundations of computation and practical techniques for implementation and application in computer systems.

Core Focus Areas

  • Programming & Software Development
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Computer Architecture & Operating Systems
  • Database Management Systems (DBMS)
  • Computer Networks & Cybersecurity
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Web & Mobile App Development
  • Cloud Computing & DevOps

Skills You Will Develop

  • Analytical and logical thinking
  • Full stack development
  • Team collaboration with Git and Agile
  • Debugging and optimization
  • UI/UX design understanding
  • Project management

Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer
  • Data Analyst / Data Scientist
  • AI/ML Engineer
  • Cybersecurity Analyst
  • Cloud Solutions Architect
  • DevOps Engineer
  • UI/UX Designer

Higher Studies & Certifications

  • M.Tech/MS in advanced computing fields
  • MBA for tech-management careers
  • AWS, Azure, Google Cloud Certifications
  • Java, Python, CCNA, Security+ Certifications

Industries Hiring CSE Graduates

  • IT & Software (Infosys, TCS, Wipro)
  • E-commerce (Amazon, Flipkart)
  • Startups (Razorpay, Swiggy, Zomato)
  • Tech Giants (Google, Microsoft, Meta)
  • Finance & Banking (JPMorgan, Goldman Sachs)
  • Government & Research (ISRO, DRDO)
