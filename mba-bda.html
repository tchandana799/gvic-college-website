About the Program

The MBA in Big Data Analytics at GVIC equips students with cutting-edge skills in data interpretation, visualization, and strategic decision-making. This program is designed for aspiring managers who want to transform businesses using large-scale data technologies.

Key Features

  • Learn Hadoop, Python, SQL, and Spark
  • Hands-on projects with real-world datasets
  • Internships & industry collaborations
  • Data-driven decision-making frameworks
  • Special focus on AI & machine learning integration

Career Opportunities

Upon completing this program, graduates can work as:

  • Data Analyst / Scientist
  • Business Intelligence Consultant
  • Analytics Project Manager
  • Market Research Analyst
  • Big Data Engineer
