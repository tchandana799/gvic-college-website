PG ECE – VLSI & Embedded Systems

Design the future of electronics with advanced embedded and VLSI technologies

Course Overview

This program aims to produce skilled engineers with a deep understanding of Very-Large-Scale Integration (VLSI) and Embedded System design. Students learn both hardware and software aspects of embedded computing systems and semiconductor design.

What You Will Learn

  • VLSI Design Principles & CAD Tools
  • ASIC/FPGA Architecture & Programming
  • Embedded Systems Design & RTOS
  • Low Power & High-Speed Circuit Design
  • Microcontrollers, ARM Architecture, IoT

Skills You Develop

  • Verilog / VHDL Coding
  • System-on-Chip (SoC) Development
  • PCB Design & Simulation
  • Embedded C & Real-time OS
  • Signal Processing with Embedded Systems

Career Opportunities

  • VLSI Design Engineer
  • FPGA Developer
  • Embedded Software Developer
  • ASIC Verification Engineer
  • IoT System Architect

The PG program in ECE with a focus on VLSI and Embedded Systems equips students with the knowledge to design and innovate in the microelectronics domain. With increasing demand in consumer electronics, defense, medical devices, and automation industries, this program is ideal for engineers who want to pursue core technology roles. Students work on state-of-the-art labs, industry projects, and learn from experienced faculty. The curriculum is continuously updated to reflect the latest in chip design and embedded computing trends.
